Between the Sheets
2 ½ Lemon juice
2 ½ Grönstedts Cognac
2 ½ Cointreau ®
2 ½ Bacardi ®
Shake with ice, serve in cocktail glass.
Brandy milk
2 tsp Powdered sugar
3 Grönstedts Cognac
Fill up with fat milk and stir fervently!
Gin Fizz
2 tsp Powdered sugar
2 Lemon juice
4 Gin
Shake with ice, then fill up with Club Soda,
and serve in collins glass with straw.
Gin Flip
2 Eggs
3 tsp Powdered sugar
8 Gin
Shake with ice, serve in collins glass with nutmeg and straw.
Margarita
4 Tequila
2 Cointreau ®
6 Lime juice
Mix with ice, serve in cocktail glass.
Milch der Grauen
2 tsp Brown sugar
3 Black Bacardi ®
Fill up with fat milk and stir fervently!
Moscow Mule
3 Lemon juice
6 Vodka
Stir with ice, then fill up with Ginger Ale,
and serve in collins glass.
